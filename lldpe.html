LLDPE(Linear Low- Density Polyethylene)

LLDPE

IT CAN BE USED IN:
                      LLDPE is a flexible, durable thermoplastic known for its excellent tensile strength and chemical resistance. Key features include

Flexibility:
                       Highly stretchable and impact-resistant. Applications: Commonly used in packaging films, bags, and containers. Versatility: Ideal for agricultural films and geomembranes.

REMARK:
                       LLDPE is favored for itsperformance in various industrial andconsumerapplications.

FOR SITE:
                       Packaging Films: Stretch and shrink wraps. Bags: Grocery, retail, and trash bags. Containers: Flexible food packaging and bottles. Agricultural Films: Greenhouse covers and mulch. Geomembranes: Landfill liners and water containment. LLDPE is valued for its flexibility, strength, and durability.
